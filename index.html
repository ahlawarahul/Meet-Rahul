Key Tenets for Technology Managers.

...As I see it.

Have right level of research and data to drive big technology decisions.

Understand the needs of the actual users of the technology.

Be able to go above and beyond to progress challenging situations through ownership.

How you deliver is as important as the technology you deliver.

Technology alone cannot solve anything without the right operational processes.

Effective Communication and Coordination is key to success.
